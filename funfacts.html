Fun Facts About Quokkas

1. They are nocturnal, like bats.

2. They are illegal to handle.

3. They are related to wallabies and kangaroos.

4. They are the smallest of the macropods, which include kangaroos and wallabies (like Rocko from Rocko's Modern Life).
